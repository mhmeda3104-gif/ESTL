Luminary began in 2016 from a small studio with a team of three who believed that beauty and function are not opposites, but two sides of the same coin.

Today, we host a diverse team of designers, developers, and strategists who work as one cohesive fabric to deliver unforgettable digital experiences.

Our journey with every client begins with deep listening and ends with a long-term partnership.
